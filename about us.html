Problem statement

Inefficient waste management and low recycling rates in urban areas contribute to environmental pollution, climate change and public health issue.

Purpose of eco cycle

Educates citizens on proper recycling practices through a mobile app or gamification

Streamlines the recycling process using smart bins with sensors and AI-powered sorting tech

Encourages community involvement through a reward system and social sharing features

Provides valuable insights to municipalities and waste management companies through data analytics

Impact of eco cycle

  • Increased recycling rates
  • Reduced waste sent to landfills
  • Improved community engagement
  • Data-driven decision making for waste management

Innovative features

  • AI-powered sorting technology
  • IOT sensors for real-time monitoring
  • Gamification and reward system
  • Data analytics platform

Beneficiaries of eco cycle

  • Community members
  • Municipalities especially local governments and waste management authorities
  • Local businesses
  • Schools and educational institutions
  • Environment

The project has the potential to make a significant impact on the environment and community by promoting sustainable practices, reducing waste and fostering a culture of recycling
